  • Triangle Shell

    The outer boundary in teal — representing structure, framework, and the visible container of the holding company.

    The mathematical grid based on φ (1.618) — the invisible foundation that enables precise growth.

Key Specifications

  • Background

    Transparent background with dark color variants: teal-dark (#2a8a8a) and amber-dark (#b8895a). This ensures visibility on both light and dark browser tabs.

  • Shape

    Square format with the symbol centered. The logo's 107×93 viewBox is placed in a 120×120 square with equal padding.

  • Spiral

    Must be a single continuous path (no gaps between arc segments). All 6 golden ratio arcs connected.

  • Colors

    Uses darker variants of the brand colors for contrast on any background. Triangle and scaffolding use #2a8a8a, spiral uses #b8895a.
