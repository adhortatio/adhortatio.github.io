Artificial Intelligence

Most see AI as another tool—faster spreadsheets, smarter search. They're wrong. This is humanity building something that reasons. We've done this once before: we called it education, and it took generations. Now it takes months.

The gap between those who understand this and those who don't isn't closing. It's compounding. Every delay widens the distance—not linearly, but exponentially.

Longevity

The immortal jellyfish reverses its aging process—a biological reality that challenges everything we assumed about the boundaries of life. If nature permits such possibilities, what else remains undiscovered?

Longevity science is not about living forever. It is about extending healthspan, compressing morbidity, and questioning limits we accepted without evidence.
